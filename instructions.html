Instructions:

  1. There are 16 displayed cards which are faced down.
  2. On each turn, a player turns over any two cards, one at a time, and keeps them if the cards match, in this case two same cards.
  3. If they successfully match a pair they get a point, and that player gets another turn.
  4. When a player turns over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again, in the same position, and it becomes the next playerâ€™s turn.
  5. The trick is to remember which cards are where.
  6. The person with the most points at the end of the game wins.
